## 1. Project Scope & Technical Requirements
The objective is to remove existing wallcoverings installed in 1987 and replace them with a modern equivalent that replicates the original "printed line" aesthetic (high visual interest vertical striae).

*   **Removal:** Surgical removal of a single layer of 30-year-old wallpaper. Installer must assume clay-based adhesive has bonded to the drywall.[1]
*   **Surface Preparation:** **Mandatory Level 5 Skim Coat.** The new pattern (vertical pinstripe) acts as a ruler; the surface must be glass-smooth and primed with a high-performance sealer (e.g., Zinsser Gardz or Roman RX-35) to prevent bubbling.
*   **Installation:** Expert hanging of either 54" commercial vinyl or 27" residential non-woven. 
    *   **Mandatory Technique:** "Reverse Hanging" (alternating every other strip 180°) to eliminate seam shading and paneling common in vertical textures.
*   **Safety/Resident Comfort:** Use only **Zero-VOC adhesives** (e.g., Roman PRO-880). No chemical strippers; steam stripping only to protect the respiratory health of the 97-year-old resident.

## 2. Quantitative Surface Analysis
Calculations are based on the Huntington Pointe **Chardonnay Model** floor plan (1,728 sq. ft.) and site-specific orientation including the Refrigerator Bay.[2, 3]

### A. Gross Preparation Area (Computation)
Used for removal and skim-coat labor estimation.
*   **Formula:** $Area_{Gross} = (Perimeter_{Main} + Perimeter_{Bay}) \times Height_{Ceiling}$
*   **Main Perimeter:** $(14' \text{ L} + 8' \text{ W}) \times 2 = 44 \text{ linear feet}$.
*   **Bay Perimeter:** $3' \text{ Back} + 2.5' \text{ Right Side} = 5.5 \text{ linear feet}$.
*   **Total Volume:** $49.5 \text{ lin ft} \times 8' \text{ Height} = \mathbf{396 \text{ Gross Sq. Ft.}}$

### B. Net Installable Area (Target Coverage)
Used for final material ordering and installation labor.
*   **Formula:** $Area_{Net} = Area_{Gross} - (Area_{Cabinets} + Area_{Openings})$
*   **Architectural Deductions:** Cabinet footprint (~132 sq ft) + Pass-through window and Pantry doors (~52 sq ft) + Eat-in slider window 4'×4' (~16 sq ft).
*   **Net Target:** $396 - (132 + 68) = \mathbf{196 \text{ Net Sq. Ft.}}$
